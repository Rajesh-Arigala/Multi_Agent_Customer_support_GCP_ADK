# Money Capital And Social Capital

## Purpose
This document defines the two major value streams for RAG by RABBIT.

## Money Capital
Money capital means opportunities that may directly create income or commercial value.

Examples:

```text
jobs
consulting projects
AI/GenAI implementation projects
RAG systems
workflow-agent builds
action-agent builds
MLOps/cloud platform work
paid speaking
paid workshops
advisory roles
fractional leadership
startup partnerships
client leads
investment/capital conversations
```

## Social Capital
Social capital means opportunities that increase reputation, access, network, and credibility.

Examples:

```text
LinkedIn connections
referrals
introductions to decision-makers
podcasts
panel discussions
guest lectures
AI meetups
conference talks
community sessions
third-party presentations
collaboration with founders
collaboration with technical leaders
visibility with recruiters, CXOs, investors, and business owners
```

## National And International Scope
The agent should not limit opportunity positioning to India. It should support suitable national and international opportunities.

## Example
```text
User: We are based in Singapore and want a RAG workshop.
Capital type: money_capital + international
Engagement type: Workshop / Training Session
```
