# Usecase 2: RAG by RABBIT - Business Use Case

## Business Goal
Build RAG by RABBIT as an action-oriented professional opportunity agent for `rajesharigala.com`. The agent should answer stakeholder questions with evidence, show that the system itself was built by Rajesh Arigala, qualify professional intent, and route national and international money-capital and social-capital opportunities.

## Product Name
Public-facing name:

```text
RAG by RABBIT
```

Expanded/internal name:

```text
RABBIT Agent GALA
```

Meaning:

```text
RAG = Retrieval-Augmented Generation
RAG = RABBIT Agent GALA
GALA = wordplay from AriGALA
```

## Target Stakeholders
- Recruiters and HR teams
- Hiring managers and decision-makers
- Consultants and potential clients
- Business collaborators and startup founders
- Investors and capital contacts
- Event organizers, podcast hosts, and panel coordinators
- Training, workshop, and guest lecture organizers
- National and international professional stakeholders

## Business Problem
Professional websites often answer "who is this person?" but fail to convert interest into structured opportunities. Visitors may be interested in hiring, consulting, speaking, collaboration, or introductions, but leave without creating an actionable lead.

## Proposed Solution
RAG by RABBIT should:

- answer from Rajesh's website and project evidence
- position the agent itself as live proof-of-work
- classify stakeholder intent
- separate money-capital and social-capital opportunities
- create engagement tickets
- prepare contact summaries
- route high-value opportunities to Rajesh

## Core Journey
```text
Visitor asks about Rajesh
-> agent answers with RAG evidence
-> agent explains that this system itself is built by Rajesh
-> visitor shows intent
-> agent classifies engagement type
-> agent collects contact/context details
-> agent creates engagement ticket
-> Rajesh receives a structured opportunity summary
```

## Business Value
- Converts passive website traffic into opportunity flow.
- Creates national and international money-capital leads.
- Creates social-capital opportunities such as talks, panels, podcasts, and introductions.
- Demonstrates Rajesh's RAG, workflow-agent, action-agent, and business-facing GenAI capability live.
- Creates a bridge from RABBIT Assistant to Sentinel Gateway.

## One-Line Summary
RAG by RABBIT turns Rajesh Arigala's professional website into a living proof-of-work opportunity engine that answers with evidence, qualifies stakeholder intent, and routes national and international money-capital and social-capital opportunities.
