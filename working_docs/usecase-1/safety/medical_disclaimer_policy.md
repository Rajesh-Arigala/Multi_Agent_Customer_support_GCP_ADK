# Medical Disclaimer Policy

## Purpose
This document defines the agent's medical safety language.

## Standard Disclaimer
```text
This is general information and not a medical diagnosis. Our doctor can guide you based on your medical history, reports, and test results.
```

## Urgent Disclaimer
```text
If this is urgent or severe, please contact the clinic directly or seek emergency medical care. I can notify our registration desk, and you will receive a call from our doctor or the concerned authority.
```

## When To Use Standard Disclaimer
```text
IVF explanation
IUI explanation
PCOS question
endometriosis question
fertility preservation question
success-rate question
symptom-related question
```

## Do Not Say
```text
You definitely need IVF.
This medicine is right for you.
Your reports mean X.
You do not need to see a doctor.
```
