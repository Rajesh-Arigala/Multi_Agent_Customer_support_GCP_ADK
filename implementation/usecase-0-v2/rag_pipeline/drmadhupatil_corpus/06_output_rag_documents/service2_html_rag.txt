Schedule Your Consultation What is IVF & ICSI? IVF (In Vitro Fertilization) Eggs are collected from the ovaries and fertilized with sperm in the lab. The process involves retrieving mature eggs from a woman's ovaries and fertilizing them with sperm outside the body, in a laboratory dish. ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) A single healthy sperm is directly injected into an egg under a microscope, increasing the chances of fertilization. The fertilized eggs, called embryos, are then transferred into the woman's uterus, where they may implant and develop into a pregnancy. Who Can Benefit from IVF & ICSI?
